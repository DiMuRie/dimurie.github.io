Victoria Paradox-light novel WIP info

Characters

-The shadow of justice

Real name:Yoshita Ito

Age:88

Role:Superhero

1 Year after his birth his parents realized he had superpowers when his parents suddenly couldnt move everytime he told them to do anything.After that incident each year he discovered another superpower,but the toll for using those superpowers was consuming emotions from humans nearby.He decided at the age of 55 after a lot of planning to create a special place in the middle of nowhere in europe where he could create an controlled enviroment with unfortunate humans made to think they were part of a victorian era rich family.

-Mistress Maria

Age:33

Original age:27

Real name:Shikuro Sagiri

Role:Female family head

A homeless woman that used to be good looking ran away from home with a the man she loved,who was a criminal that only valued her beauty.After disobeying him countless times he started disfiguring her,until she was unrecognizable.Children in the village near their improvised home would run away from her letting blood curdling screams,Leaving her traumatised until she was mentally broken.The shadow saw the state the woman was in and took pity upon her,making her beautiful as she used to be,erasing her memories and implanting new ones.She is now the wife of the head of the artificial family
